Introduction

Meet Candy. She's not just a dog, she's a force of nature, a professional snuggler, and a certified happiness expert.

Candy believes that every lap is meant to be sat on (regardless of size constraints), every tear deserves a thorough licking, and every moment is an opportunity for tail wags and belly rubs.

About Candy

Candy came into this world with one mission: to spread love and chaos in equal measure. With her soulful eyes and that magnificent snoot, she's mastered the art of getting exactly what she wants-usually treats, walks, and more treats.

She's a professional napper, an amateur landscaper (your garden will never be the same), and holds a black belt in the ancient art of counter surfing. Despite weighing as much as a small child, she's convinced she's a lap dog and will attempt to prove this at every opportunity.

Her hobbies include staring contests with squirrels, eating apples, and providing emotional support whether you asked for it or not.

Contact

Want to schedule a playdate? Need some wisdom? Looking for a snuggle buddy in town? Candy is always available for pets, treats, and meaningful conversations (she's a great listener).

You can usually find her napping in her bed, waiting by the kitchen for food, or dramatically sighing because you've been on your phone for more than 10 minutes.

Bring me an apple and I shall bless you for eternity... after eating it.
